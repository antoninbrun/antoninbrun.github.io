About me
I am a first-year civil engineering PhD student at i-lab, part of the Viterbi School of Engineering at USC, working on Human-Building Interaction. I am advised by Burçin Becerik-Gerber.

I am passionate about wellbeing in the built environment, building energy efficiency, and intelligent occupant-centred design.

I have an MS in Civil and Environmental Engineering (focus in Sustainable Design and Construction) from Stanford University where I was a graduate researcher for Catherine Gorlé and a public speaking teaching assistant for Matt Vassar (ENGR103/203). I also have a B.Eng. in Bioresource Engineering from McGill University.
